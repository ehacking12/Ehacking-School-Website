Our Story

Founded in 2020, Ehacking School emerged from a vision to democratize cybersecurity education. Our founders recognized a critical gap in the market: millions of aspiring security professionals lacked access to quality, practical training from industry experts.

What started as a small initiative with just 3 instructors has grown into a thriving community of over 50,000 students across 120+ countries. We've trained security professionals now working at Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and innovative startups worldwide.

Today, we remain committed to our core mission: delivering industry-relevant, practical cybersecurity education that transforms careers and protects organizations from evolving threats.
